Where Are The Plastic Bags is a call to track down all the roaming plastic bags on the loose. Plastic waste is the biggest culprit in oceanic degradation and a problem that literally grows by the minute. The misconception of the difference between curbside recycing and and flexible film recycling only contributes to the problem more. We hope that by creating a conscious awareness of plastic bags and their pervasive presence, then awareness can translate into the correct recycling practices and eventual discontinued plastic bag use.
